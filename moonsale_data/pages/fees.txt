# Platform Fees

=== CONTENT ===
All fees charged by MoonSale. Admin updates are reflected here automatically.
Listing fees are paid once after deploying your project, in the native token of your chosen chain. Platform fees are enforced on-chain at finalization and are fully verifiable on-chain. No hidden fees.
